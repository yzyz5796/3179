Household Income and Poverty in Malaysia

The map above shows the median household income by state in Malaysia for the year 2022. States with higher median incomes are represented by darker shades. This visualization helps identify disparities in income distribution across different regions.

Income & Poverty Insights

From the visualizations, we can observe the distribution of household income and poverty rates across various states in Malaysia. These insights can help policy makers address inequalities and identify areas that need the most attention. Further analysis can also reveal the impact of regional factors on income disparities.
